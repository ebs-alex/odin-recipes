Hydration Health Drink


This drink helps with hydrations, oxygenization and parasite removal.
Baking soda among many other beneifts helps with oxygenization.
Glycene is a great supplement with many benefits including cognitive function.
Diotamaceous earth helps kill parasites in the gut.
Carbonated water brings it all together into a suprisingly refreshing and not at all untasteful super health drink.

    Ingredients

  • 1-2 cans of quality sparkling water
  • teaspoon of baking soda
  • teaspon of glycine powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of FOOD GRADE diaotamaceous earth poweder
  • half stick of "Liquid IV" hydration powder
  • If no Liquid IV powder available sub with teaspoon of sugar or honey

    Steps

  1. Pour sparkling water into a cup with 1/4 space leftover. let fizz settle.
  2. Add baking soda. let fizz settle.
  3. Add glycine powder. let fizz settle.
  4. Add diaotamaceous earth. let fizz settle.
  5. Gentle mix.

    6. Enjoy.
