Alex Latte

The best damn latte you'll ever have

    Ingredients

  • Grounded Espresso beans
  • Milk or creamer
  • pinch of butter
  • 3 pinches of cinnamon
  • teaspon or tablespoon of honey
  • 2 pinches of salt

    Steps

  1. Grind espresso beans if not aleady Grounded
  2. Prep espresso machine
  3. pour honey to bottom of empty cup
  4. Add your 2 pinches of salt on top of honey
  5. Add 2 pinches of cinnamon to honey
  6. Add pinch of butter to honey
  7. Commence espresso machine, output into prepped cup
  8. Add last pinch of cinnamon to espresso
  9. Froth 1/4 cup of Milk
  10. Pour frothed milk slowly into espresso

    11. Enjoy.
